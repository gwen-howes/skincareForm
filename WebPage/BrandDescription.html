Our Story

The Beginning

In the serene countryside of Northern California, nestled between rolling hills and lush meadows, the seeds of Purely You Skincare were sown. Our founder, Gwendolyn Howes, a passionate herbalist and skincare enthusiast, grew up surrounded by the wonders of nature. Inspired by her grandmother's age-old beauty recipes and her own commitment to natural living, Gwendolyn embarked on a journey to create skincare products that were not only effective but also kind to the environment and gentle on the skin.

A Passion for Purity

In 2010, armed with a degree in botanical sciences and a dream, Gwendolyn started experimenting with various natural ingredients in her kitchen. She believed that skincare should be simple, pure, and derived from nature’s finest offerings. Her first creations were small batches of moisturizers and serums, made from organic herbs and plant extracts sourced from local farms. Friends and family were the first to experience the magic of these formulations, and their glowing feedback spurred Gwendolyn to take her hobby to the next level.

Growing a Community

Word of mouth quickly spread about the effectiveness and purity of Gwendolyn’s products. In 2012, Purely You Skincare was officially born. With a commitment to transparency, sustainability, and cruelty-free practices, the brand rapidly gained a loyal following. Gwendolyn's small kitchen lab evolved into a full-fledged production facility, but her dedication to handcrafted quality remained unwavering. Every product continued to be made with love, in small batches, ensuring the highest level of care and potency.

Innovating with Nature

As the brand grew, so did Gwendolyn's team. A group of like-minded individuals, including botanists, skincare experts, and environmental advocates, joined her mission. Together, they expanded the product line, introducing innovative solutions for various skin concerns, all while staying true to the brand’s core values. From anti-aging elixirs to soothing balms for sensitive skin, each product was meticulously formulated with the finest natural ingredients and backed by scientific research.

Our Commitment

Today, Purely You Skincare is a beacon of natural beauty, trusted by thousands who seek radiant, healthy skin without compromising on their values. We remain committed to sustainability, using eco-friendly packaging, and supporting organic farming practices. Our journey is guided by a simple philosophy: that the best skincare solutions are found in nature, and everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin.

Looking Ahead

As we continue to grow, we pledge to maintain our dedication to purity, transparency, and environmental stewardship. The future holds exciting possibilities, and we are thrilled to have you with us on this journey. Thank you for believing in Purely You Skincare, where nature meets science, and beauty is always pure.
